Get To Know Dr Janique

Meet Dr Janique

I studied at the University of Johannesburg, South Africa, which is also accredited under the European Council of Chiropractic. Nearing the end of my studies, I began to research various other established Chiropractors in search of a mentor; someone who would guide me both professionally and personally. It was at this time that I was referred to a well known Chiropractor in the northern suburbs who showed me different techniques and enhancing my chiropractic profession extensively.

Who can I Treat

I am focused on the health and wellness of the Whole Family, from infants to the elderly, athletes and sportsmen. I specialise in the care for pregnant mothers and children of all ages. Come in as a family - I would love to have you.

Mission Statement

With passion and compassion, “My Mission” is to strive to help others by chiropractic and wellness care achieving health, happiness, and fulfilment through a positive and mental attitude, patient education, and chiropractic care, maintaining the highest ethics, integrity, and professional standards—never ceasing to improve my services at every level, today, tomorrow, and for generations to come. Enabling patients with the information and inspiration needed to choose optimal health solutions for themselves and their families.

Vision Statement

I believe everyone deserves the highest quality of a pain free healthy life. My philosophy is to offer my patients the very best optimal personalised care, by achieving and maintaining a healthy life accompanied by impeccable service in a comfortable environment. My goal is that my patients leave my office empowered with a better understanding of the chiropractic treatment they received and a greater knowledge of the functioning of their body.
